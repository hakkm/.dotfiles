* Files Pdf

  =convert *.jpg out.pdf=
  or 
  =convert img1.jpg img2.jpg out.pdf=
** rotate a photo 
  =convert -rotate 90 img.jpg out.jpg=
  or 
  =convert img.jpg -rotate 180 out.jpg=
  90 => 90 degrees clockwise, and so on
